services

Our Services

We pride ourselves on making things simple for you. We offer everything from custom software to content generation to website management. We build your digital presence so you don't have to.

Custom Software

We are passionate about creating exceptional software. Whether you need a custom data-mining application, a way for your users to log input via a text message or anything else, we are delighted to provide a solution to your problems.

Websites

Every year the proportion of people who use hand held devices like mobile phones and tablet computers increases. With this in mind we design all our websites to look and function equally well, no matter how you access it.

E-Commerce

We understand the difference between a shopping experience that will bore your customers and one that will wow them. We have experience integrating with Shopify or building you a beautiful custom solution so your customers can easily shop online.

Logo Design

A memorable logo will increase loyalty and help to bring your cusomters back. Our designers, who won't be satisfied until you are, will work with you to bring your vision to life.

Content Creation

We have the know-how and expertise to create content that is both meaningful and interesting to your users. Through extensive market research and close collaboration, we will come up with a content creation strategy that works for you.

Website Management

We are happy to manage your website so you can stick to the important job of running your business. We will always respond to service issues as soon as we receive them. No downtime for you.

Analytics

Anaylzing who comes to your site, which devices they use to access your pages, when they choose to visit... these are all facts which truly allow you to understand your target market. We will gladly build you a custom solution so you too can track and better understand who uses your site.

Social Media Consulting

Need a facebook page? Not sure what to tweet about? We design social media strategies so you can stay on top of the latest digital trends.
